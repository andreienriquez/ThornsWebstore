DESCRIPTION

Thorn is a cutting-edge hip hop clothing brand that embodies the raw energy, individuality, and rebellious spirit of street culture. Born from the streets and inspired by the boldness of hip hop, Thorn creates high-impact, fashion-forward apparel that speaks to those who dare to stand out and speak their truth.

Our designs push the limits of style with bold graphics, fearless patterns, and distinct, urban silhouettes. From oversized hoodies and graphic tees to statement accessories, Thorn is for the hustlers, the creators, and the dreamers who embrace their own unique journey. Every piece is crafted with quality, comfort, and attitude, designed not just to wear, but to represent a lifestyle.

At Thorn, we believe in the power of self-expression and authenticity. Our clothing isn’t just about trends; it's about standing tall, being unapologetic, and letting your style tell your story. Just like the thorn itself—sharp, resilient, and strong—our brand is about breaking through barriers, challenging norms, and carving your own path in the world.

With a commitment to quality craftsmanship and sustainability, Thorn is more than a clothing brand; it’s a movement. Join us in redefining streetwear. Stay sharp. Stay real. Stay Thorn.

Objectives

Engage with Culture: Stay deeply connected to the heartbeat of hip hop culture, celebrating its influence in fashion, music, and the street scene, while supporting emerging artists and movements.

Lead in Innovation: Continuously push the boundaries of design and craftsmanship, creating apparel that is not only on trend but also timeless and versatile.

Promote Self-Expression: Provide a platform for individuals to express their personal style, confidence, and individuality through our distinctive clothing collections.

MISSION & VISION

Our vision is to be a trailblazer in hip hop-inspired fashion in the Philippines, recognized for pushing the boundaries of creativity and design. We envision a community where individuals confidently wear their stories, embracing their true selves with style that celebrates authenticity, diversity, and resilience. Thorn aims to bridge the gap between fashion, culture, and sustainability, becoming a symbol of self-expression for the Filipino youth and empowering them to stand out in their own unique way.
